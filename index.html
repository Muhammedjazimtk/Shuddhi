About Us

Shuddhi is helping your community reach its sustainability goals with a revolutionary solution in waste diversion.

We can help you achieve your waste diversion goals, turning the unrecyclable into the reclaimed.

  • Our curbside trash and recycling pickup is the best choice for your home, and the environment.
  • Need removal fast? No problem. We offer same/next day appointments and waste removal on-the-spot.
  • If your items still have life, we'll take them to the nearest charity or recycling depot.
